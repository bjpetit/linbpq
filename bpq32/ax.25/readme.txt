Ethereal Protocol Decoder Plugin for BPQEther/AXIP/AXUDP


This provides a plugin for Ethereal to decode Amateur Radio AX.25 and NET/ROM packets 
encapsulated in BPQEther, AX/IP (IP Protocol 93) or AX/UDP (udp port 10093). 

Copy ax.25.dll to your Ethereal plugins folder.
